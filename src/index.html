About the festival


WonderLand music festival, as with other “lands", was not built all at once. The first memories date from 2013, when a few friends at a party decided to create their own music festival, to be known as – Territory of Wonderful People! At the beginning, WonderLand was held on Trash Island at the Pacific Ocean. But who can hold free people to one place? In 2014, WonderLand moved to the airport “Windonsen”. Every year, the land was growing, and the number of its citiziens too. In 2017, it was decided to move WonderLand to the East Coast of New-York. The number of WonderLand people had grown from 1500 in the first year, to 16,000 unique visitors over four days in 2017.
